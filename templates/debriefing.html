Debriefing

Thank you for your participation in our study! Your anonymous data makes an important contribution to our understanding of human decision making.

The purpose of this experiment was to determine the optimality of human decision making. In life we are expected to choose between 'exploring' and 'exploiting. This dichotomy transcends species, birds determine which bushes to feed at through exploring before exploiting the most valuable option. So it is with humans, we search for jobs (exploring) before choosing (exploiting). This experiment aimed to simulate this paradigm and your participation will contribute towards understanding the optimality and process of human decision making. Thank you.
